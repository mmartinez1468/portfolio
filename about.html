I could sense myself sinking into a place I didn’t want to be, and the more I ignored it, the deeper I fell. It was then that I realized something crucial: if I didn’t take action soon, I might never recover.
I finally made the decision to dedicate myself to learning how to program, and the progress I’ve made so far has been incredible. Thanks to resources like The Odin Project, combined with my schoolwork and a job in an IT department, I’ve been able to consistently improve and grow in my skills.
